This is a guide to create a simple Resume

Your resume must atleast include the folowing

  1. Personal details
    • Title (Mr.,Ms.,Prof. etc.)
    • Name
    • Date of Birth
    • Gender
    • Any other details you prefer

  2. A quick summary

    This part will be a very short Introduction about yourself, your qualities and skills.
    Keep it very professional and short.


  3. Educational qualifications
    • Name of the institution
    • Name of the course
    • Your grades

      • You can include details about any other courses you completed online

  4. Work experience

    5. This part must be very clear. it is the deciding factor of your job. Try including the following details for a crisp review of your past jobs:

    • Name of the company/ industry/ institution
    • Peiod of service (Date of join and date of resignation)
    • Your work

      • It basically includes what what work you performed and in what feild you worked in. (Make sure you include bullet pointers to make it more precise)

  5. Skills

    6. If you are a fresher and have no work experience then this part of your resume must shout out loud. You can include the following skills:

    • Communication skills
    • Basic skills related to your field

  6. Awards and Certifications (if any)

    7. Try including the awards or certificates more relevant to your field.

  7. Include your hobbies
